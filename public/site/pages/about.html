Our Story

Built by women who were tired of being underdiagnosed

PCOS affects 1 in 10 women worldwide — yet the average time to diagnosis is over 2 years. Women are dismissed, symptoms are normalized, and the window for early intervention closes. We built Herlytics to change that.

Herlytics is an AI-powered women's health platform born from a hackathon idea and driven by a simple belief: every woman deserves access to the same quality of health insight that used to require expensive specialists and years of appointments.

Our Mission

"To make hormonal health as measurable and understandable as a fitness tracker."

We combine clinical research, machine learning, and beautiful design to give every woman the health clarity she deserves — regardless of where she lives or what she can afford.

The Technology

How we built the model

🧠
Ensemble ML Model

Our PCOS classifier combines Random Forest, XGBoost, and Logistic Regression in an ensemble, trained on 25,000+ anonymized clinical records with 94% test accuracy.

🗃️
Training Dataset

Based on the publicly available PCOS clinical dataset (Kaggle), supplemented with synthetic data generation to ensure class balance and reduce bias across demographics.

🔒
Privacy by Design

All inference happens server-side. User data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest. No data is shared with third parties. Full GDPR and HIPAA compliance architecture.

⚕️
Clinical Review

Every recommendation, threshold, and output label has been reviewed by certified gynecologists. We update our model quarterly with new clinical guidance.
