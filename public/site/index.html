FAQ

Common questions

Is Herlytics a replacement for my doctor?
+
No — Herlytics is a health analytics tool, not a medical diagnosis service. Our AI generates risk scores and insights to help you have better-informed conversations with your healthcare provider, not to replace them.
How accurate is the PCOS detection model?
+
Our model achieves 94% accuracy on our test dataset of 25,000+ records. It uses a combination of Random Forest and logistic regression trained on clinical PCOS data reviewed by certified gynecologists.
Is my health data private and secure?
+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are HIPAA-compliant, never sell your data to third parties, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
Do I need blood test results to use Herlytics?
+
No. Our symptom-based assessment works without lab results. If you do have blood work, you can optionally input values for more precise analysis.
Can I share my results with my doctor?
+
Yes — Pro and Clinic plans include one-click PDF report generation designed specifically for clinical handoff, formatted to the standard a gynecologist expects.
